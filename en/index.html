Learning Circles are:


Free

Learning Circles are lightly-facilitated study groups for individuals who want to take online courses together, in-person. Built on open source technology and utilizing free online educational resources such as MOOCs, Learning Circles connect individuals directly to high-quality learning experiences, for free.

Social

Learning Circles provide a social wrapper for online learning, supported by a designated facilitator (who could be you!). Far more than just delivering content, the Circles support the development of interpersonal skills and provide a space for participants to meaningfully engage with both online educational resources and with their peers.

Local

Delivered locally, Learning Circles bring online learning back down to Earth. By identifying courses and subject matter relevant to specific communities and marketing the Circles through offline channels, Learning Circles engage individuals who wouldnâ€™t otherwise benefit from online education and transform physical spaces into community learning hubs.

Yours

We believe that anybody can facilitate a Learning Circle, and so we have developed a Learning Circle toolkit, complete with software tools, facilitation guides, curated course lists, and marketing templates. Whether you want to run just one Learning Circle in your home or organize 100 across an urban library system, the toolkit is the place to start.
