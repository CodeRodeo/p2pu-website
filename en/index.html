About P2PU

Born out of the 2008 Cape Town Open Education Declaration, P2PU is a US-based non-profit organization that cultivates peer-learning communities in public spaces around the world. Since 2015, we have worked with libraries worldwide to realize our mission and vision through a methodology called learning circles.
