Introducing Learning Circles

Join a Learning Circle Run a Learning Circle

Learning Circles are peer-led study groups for learners who want to take online courses together and in-person.

Learning Circles are:


Social

Learning Circles provide a social wrapper for online learning, supported by a designated facilitator (who could be you!). Far more than just delivering content, Learning Circles support the development of interpersonal skills and provide a space for participants to meaningfully engage with both online educational resources and with their peers.

Local

Delivered locally, Learning Circles bring online learning down to Earth. By identifying courses and subject matter relevant to specific communities and marketing through offline channels, Learning Circles engage individuals who wouldnâ€™t otherwise benefit from online education and transform physical spaces into community learning hubs.

Free

Built on open source technology and utilizing free online educational resources such as MOOCs, Learning Circles connect individuals directly to high-quality learning experiences, for free.

Proven

A 2015 pilot across 11 branches of Chicago Public Library demonstrated that Learning Circles can increase retention for online learning tenfold, reach entirely new audiences of first-time online learners, and foster an environment that learners, facilitators, and library staff are eager to repeat.

Yours

We believe that anybody can run a Learning Circle, and so we have developed a Learning Circle toolkit, complete with software tools, facilitation guides, curated course lists, and marketing templates. Whether you want to run just one Learning Circle in your home or organize dozens across an entire library system, the toolkit is the place to start.
