Start a Team

Teams are organized learning circle communities, typically affiliated with library systems and community-based organizations. While anyone can facilitate a learning circle for free, organizations that wish to build a thriving network of learning circles often benefit from additional support and tools.

Team membership is designed to provide comprehensive guidance via:

  • Staff Professional Development
  • 1:1 Support
  • Community-Powered OER Library
  • Software for Teams
  • Community of Practice
  • Discounted Additional Services

Team membership is offered on a sliding scale between $2,000 - $10,000 per year, calculated based on your organization's annual operating budget.
