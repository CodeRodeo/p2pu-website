Learning Circles are lightly-facilitated study groups for learners who want to take freely available online courses together, in-person. We believe that anybody can become a facilitator, and whether you want to run a single Learning Circle in your home or organize dozens across an urban library system, you've come to the right place.

You can run a Learning Circle with just this guide and an online course, but we highly recommend creating your Learning Circle with our online tools. These tools will generate a webpage where people can signup for your Learning Circle, as well as a create a facilitator dashboard from where you can communicate directly with learners via email or SMS, set up automatic reminders to go out before each Learning Circle, and share weekly feedback with learners, P2PU, and other facilitators around the world.

Now for the fun part. Start reading through this guide by navigating through the tabs to the left, or download it all as a pdf that you can read offline. Either way, you’ll see green highlights throughout that point out where our online tools can support your Learning Circle.

When you’re ready to begin, click on the button below to create your Learning Circle. You’ll have your own Learning Circle webpage ready to share in less than five minutes!
