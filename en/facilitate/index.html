Facilitate

Learning circles are free, interest-driven study groups for people who want to learn together in their neighborhoods. Each learning circle is led by a facilitator, who hosts each meeting and supports the growth of a peer learning environment. This facilitator does not have to be an expert in the subject matter, so with a little practice and training, anybody can facilitate.

Whether you want to run a single learning circle in your home or organize dozens across a library system, we are here to help. If you're new to Peer 2 Peer University and learning circles, we suggest you start by watching our introductory video.

Facilitator Resources

Here is an introduction to the collection of videos, stories, activities, templates, and discussions that will help you create and run a successful learning circle. These resources have been developed in partnership between the P2PU team and learning circle facilitators around the world.
